EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement is entered into between ABC Technologies Pvt Ltd and the Employee.

The employee agrees to perform duties assigned by the company and follow all company policies.

The company may terminate this agreement immediately in case of breach of contract or misconduct.

The employee shall indemnify and hold harmless the company against any losses, damages, or liabilities arising from negligence.

Any disputes arising out of this agreement shall be resolved through arbitration under the laws of India.

The governing law for this agreement shall be the jurisdiction of Chennai courts.

The employee shall not disclose confidential information during or after employment.

This agreement is valid for a period of two years from the date of joining.
